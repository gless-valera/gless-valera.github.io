About Me

The person behind the research

I'm Julianne Gless Valera — a UX researcher with roots in psychology, healthcare, and a deep curiosity about people. Before UX, I was on the path to medical school, working as a mental health technician and hospital intern. Empathy came first; tools came later.

A family member — a product engineer at Oakley — introduced me to a UX researcher on his team and changed my trajectory entirely. The idea of helping people through everyday products stuck with me, and I completed UC Davis's UX/UI bootcamp and never looked back.

Today I blend research rigor with creative instinct — whether I'm running usability tests, designing ad creatives, or crafting content that actually connects. I believe great UX starts with genuinely caring about the person on the other side of the screen.

When I'm not working, you can find me with my wiener dog Cocoa, exploring boba shops, traveling, or at a K-pop concert 🎶
